About Weightlifting

What Is Weightlifting?
Tableau Data

Weightlifting is a sport that involves lifting heavy weights in a competitive setting. There are two main types of weightlifting: the snatch and the clean and jerk. In the snatch, the lifter lifts the barbell from the ground to overhead in one continuous movement. In the clean and jerk, the lifter lifts the barbell from the ground to shoulder level (the clean) and then from the shoulders to overhead (the jerk). Weightlifting requires a combination of strength, power, and technique, and is a popular sport in many countries around the world.

Benefits of Weightlifting

Weightlifting has many benefits, including:

  • Increased strength and power
  • Improved body composition (increased muscle mass and decreased body fat)
  • Improved bone density
  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Improved mental health (reduced stress and anxiety)

Getting Started with Weightlifting

If you're interested in getting started with weightlifting, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  1. Start with light weights and focus on proper technique
  2. Gradually increase the weight as you get stronger
  3. Work with a qualified coach or trainer to learn proper technique and programming
  4. Be patient and consistent – progress takes time!

Famous Weightlifers

  1. Pyrros Dimas

    • Three-time Olympic gold medalist (1992, 1996, 2000)
    • Six-time world champion
    • Retired as one of the greatest weightlifters of all time

  2. Naim Süleymanoğlu

    • Three-time Olympic gold medalist (1988, 1992, 1996)
    • World record holder in several weight categories
    • Known as the "Pocket Hercules" due to his small stature and incredible strength

  3. Tamás Ajan

    • President of the International Weightlifting Federation (IWF) from 2000-2020
    • Instrumental in making weightlifting an Olympic sport for women
    • Inducted into the International Weightlifting Federation Hall of Fame in 2016
